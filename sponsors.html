Why their support matters

Students impacted

Hundreds of students participate annually—gaining teamwork skills, leadership experience, and a love of mathematics.

Interested in sponsoring?

We welcome partners who share our mission. Reach out via our contact page to learn about sponsorship tiers.

FLSAM is an organization of math competitors across the state of Florida who come together to focus on higher-level mathematics and train for contests. FLSAM has been active since 2003; it is currently incorporated and registered as a 501(c)(3).
